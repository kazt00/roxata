Drawing
and Drafting

All the ideas and designs from architects, engineers, owners, etc. shall be presented in ordered plans in order to be communicated to others. We offer the service of drafting any plan using several CAD software to achieve the accuracy and formality that industry demands.

Prior to the execution of any trade, it is necessary to know if the job to be done by subcontractors is in accordance with the integral development of the project. Shop drawings are submitted by each trade to be approved before execution. By using several CAD tools, we elaborate shop drawings with a high level of detail.

Several trades involve prefabricated items brought on site for installation. Fabrication drawings are plans that contain all the information needed for manufacturers to build those items per the approved shop drawings.

There is no single construction project that is built as originally intended. We elaborate as-built plans to reflect the reality of how the project was really built.
Cost
Estimation

Roxata counts. The core of a proper estimate is knowing the exact quantities the project contains. To do so, we use different software depending on the trade involved, to count them as accurately as possible.

As simple as that. From the take-offs, we generate the bill of materials classified as our clients prefer so they can send them to their vendors.

By deeply analyzing the scope, we compute unit costs considering the cost of materials, manpower, tools and machinery, and overhead according to local conditions.

BIDs management and submission requires a considerable amount of time once the cost estimation is ready. We take care of the complete formal process, from the beginning to the end, of digitally submitting the information required by the contracting party and doing revisions to proposals submitted.

In most cases, different suppliers will come up with different considerations for their bids for the same project. It is up to us to analyze each bid and to level them to make sure all are bidding on the same scope.
Project
Management

Construction is an orderly process. From a project´s blueprints, we generate a logical construction sequence integrating all the activities needed to achieve the final goal on schedule.

Using the input of our customers, we systematically keep track and classify all the costs of a project so the financial status of it is always clear.

We deal with the technical communications intended to approve methods, materials, and plans by the engineer and/or architect.

Today’s world relies on IT and the construction Industry is no exception. We use modeling software tools to create virtual models of projects and use the information that arose from them to facilitate the real-life construction process.

Quality is assured by recording all the documentation regarding the correct development of the construction process. We take care of that recording and assure no document will be missing when needed.
